Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about SISY, our platform tools, and how everything works together.

SISY is a visibility infrastructure platform built specifically for women experts. It connects qualified women with speaking opportunities, event organizers, podcast hosts, and media - while helping organizers find diverse, high-quality speakers beyond their existing networks. SISY is free to join and open to women experts in any field.

Visit sisy.my and follow the onboarding steps to create your expert profile. You will add your name, bio, areas of expertise, speaking topics, and links. Profiles are reviewed before they go live to ensure quality. If you need help during setup, contact hello@sisy.world.

Yes - core SISY access is free. This includes creating a profile on sisy.my, browsing opportunities on sisy.today, and accessing community resources on sisy.social. Some advanced features and courses on sisy.courses may require a subscription. We will always be transparent about what is free and what is paid.

sisy.today is a live feed of speaking opportunities, CFPs (calls for proposals), podcast guest spots, and expert visibility opportunities. You can browse by topic, format, and deadline. Organizers can also post opportunities directly. New listings are added daily.

Visit sisy.video and follow the upload instructions. You can link to existing video content (YouTube, Vimeo, Loom) or upload a new clip. Speaker reels are a key part of your SISY profile - they help organizers assess your speaking style quickly. We recommend a 2-3 minute highlight reel if you are just starting out.

Organizers can access expert profiles on sisy.my, watch speaker reels on sisy.video, and post opportunities on sisy.today. For more advanced search and filtering, visit sisy.team - our dedicated organizer tools hub. You can also email hello@sisy.world to discuss how SISY can support your event specifically.

The SISY community lives primarily on sisy.social and in the SISY Discord server. Both spaces host discussions, advice, events, and resources for women experts and allies. You can join the Discord at discord.gg/sisy and create a community account at sisy.social. Community membership is free.

Use the contact form below to reach the SISY team directly. You can also email hello@sisy.world or ask in the SISY Discord server where community members and moderators are active daily. We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours on weekdays.

Community Guidelines

SISY is built on mutual respect and a shared commitment to making expert visibility more equitable. Here is how we keep that standard high.

01

Be accurate

Only list expertise you genuinely have. Profiles that misrepresent credentials will be removed. Organizers trust our directory - let us protect that trust together.

02

Be respectful

SISY spaces are for building each other up. Harassment, gatekeeping, or dismissing others' expertise is not tolerated in any form.

03

Be generous

Share opportunities, make introductions, and amplify others. The visibility economy grows when we give as much as we take from it.

04

No spam or self-promotion overload

Your profile exists for discovery. Community spaces are for dialogue. Excessive unsolicited promotion will result in moderation action.
